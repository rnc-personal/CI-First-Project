ABOUT THE COMPANY

EARS was started in 2016 in Sweden by two graduates - Anders Jarlsberg and Nikolas Sundin.


Their goal was to provide an all-in-one solution for both new-comers to the headphone market as well as establised brands. At the time, getting a prototype made and bring a product to market was extremtly difficult. Most companies didnt even make it! Backed by a team of investors with many decades in the audio industry, EARS have gone from strength to strength, launching several of the worlds most popular headphones. From budget to super high-end audio devices, they are behind them all.

AI POWERED MANUFACTURING

We aren't boasdsting when we say that we have some of the most advanced manufacturing in the world!
